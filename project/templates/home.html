Divide and Choose for Three

A person partitions the items to 3 bundles, using approximate leximin-n-partition, and gives his peers the choice. When there are no conflicts, each of the two gets their favorite bundle. If there are conflicts, another person takes his favorite bundles (the third bundle goes to the first person) and partitions them (again with approximate leximin-n-partition) as the third member gets to choose his favorite bundle and the remaining onw goes to

Finds an MMA1 allocation for 3 agents.

MMA1 - Maximin aware by one item. If everyone gets more than you, at least it's by one item only.

A leximin n-partition maximizes the minimum value over all possible n partitions, and if there is a tie it selects the one maximizing the second minimum value, and so on.
